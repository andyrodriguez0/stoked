Our Mission:

Stoked is on a mission to combat hunger in communities through affordable, healthy, and convenient food trucks.

We believe that hunger is not just a result of financial hardship, but also limited access to affordable and nutritious food. The growing prevalence of food deserts have left communities with fewer options.

That's where Stoked comes in. Our food trucks go where others don't, and our pay-what-you-wish pricing ensures that people can eat healthy and dignified meals in their community.
